Following the completion of his PhD, Prof. How studied for two years as a postdoctoral associate for the Middeck Active Control Experiment (MACE), which flew on-board the Space Shuttle Endeavour in March 1995. Prof. How was the Raymond L. Bisplinghoff Fellow for MIT Aero/Astro Department from 2006-2008 and 2016 Fellow of the American Institute of Aeronautics and Astronautics (AIAA). Hes currently an associate fellow of AIAA, and a senior member of IEEE.
