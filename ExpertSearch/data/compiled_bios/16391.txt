I received an M.Sc in Software Engineering of Distributed Systems (SEDS) from KTH, Sweden (2015). I have worked as a software developer from 2008 - 2013. My research interests include recommender systems, deep learning and social trust (trust in online social networks). I work as a teaching assistant in two courses: Modern Methods of Software Engineering (MMSE) and Distributed Artificial Intelligence and Intelligent Agents (DAIIA). The focus of my PhD thesis is on how to combine information from visual and textual sources to provide more accurate and fine-grained recommendations, with fashion as the main application area.I have participated inthe EU HORIZON2020 projectEDSA (European Data Science Academy) and I am one of the organisers of FashionXRecsys which is aworkshop focused onfashion recommendation systemsthatis organised within the ACMRecommender Systems conference (Recsys).
