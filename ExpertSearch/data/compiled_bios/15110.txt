Previously, I was a postdoc researcher at the University of Pennsylvania. I received my Ph.D. in Statistics from the University of Wisconsin-Madison. Research InterestsMy research interests lie in the intersection of statistical methodology (nonparametric and high-dimensional statistics) and biomedical applications (microbiome and imaging).
