Current Student Transfer Student Faculty / Staff Student Veteran Parent Alumni Vikings Fan PSU Electrical and Computer Engineering Xiaoyu Song Xiaoyu Song Professor Contact: Fourth Avenue Building 160-15 (503) 725-5398 songx@pdx.edu Faculty Website:http://web.cecs.pdx.edu/~song/ Education: Ph.D. University of Pisa, Italy Research Interests: Formal methods Design automation Embedded system design Emerging technologies Profile: Professor Song received his Ph.D. degree from the University of Pisa, Pisa, Italy, 1991. From 1992 to 1999, he was a Faculty Member with the University of Montreal, QC, Canada. In 1998, he was a Senior Technical Staff with Cadence Design Systems, San Jose, CA. Since 1999, he has been with Portland State University, where he is currently a Professor with the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering.
