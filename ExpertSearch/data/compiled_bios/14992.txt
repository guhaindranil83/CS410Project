to the NAEseven since 2015 Election to theNational Academy of Engineering(NAE) is considered the greatest professional honor accorded an engineer in the United States. NAE members are national leaders distinguished in industry, research, and public policy. More than 30 Duke alumni and faculty have been elected to the Academy since its founding in 1964.
