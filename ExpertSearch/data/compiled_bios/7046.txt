Furukawa Professor and Zinn Faculty ScholarDirector of VICTOR Lab Home Find Faculty Tomonari Furukawa Bio Ph.D., University of Tokyo, 1996M.S., University of Sydney, 1993B.S., Waseda University, 1990 Professor Furukawa received hisB.Eng. in Mechanical Engineering from Waseda University, Japan, in 1990, and a M.Eng. (Research) in Mechatronic Engineering from University of Sydney, Australia, in 1993 and a Ph.D in Quantum Engineering and Systems Science from University of Tokyo, Japan, in 1996. He has extensive research experiences in the field of robotics and computational mechanics. He has published over 300 journal and refereed conference papers, served on Editorial Boards for five international journals and as an international organizing committee member for over 10 international conferences and won a number of career research awards and paper awards.
