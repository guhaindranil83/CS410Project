Award carried a cash prize of Rs50,000, citation and memento. Awarded the prestigious Dr TMA Pai Endowment Chair in Earth Sciences by Manipal Academy of Higher Education. This carries an annual grant of Rs 10 lakhs to pursue advanced research and teaching. This Chair is awarded in recognition of my scientific accomplishments. ACHIEVEMENT Member of the 37th Indian Scientific Expedition to Antarctica.
