Current projects that Professor Perkins is workingon include: synchronization and scheduling of manufacturing systems,data mining and clustering in genetic networks, and analytical solutionsof controlled queueing networks. Professor Perkins manufacturing-relatedresearch is performed in the Production Control of Manufacturing Systems(PCMS) Laboratory at Boston University. Biography Professor Perkins is an affiliated faculty with the Center for Informationand Systems Engineering (CISE) and a faculty member of the Division ofSystems Engineering. He is a member of the Institute of IndustrialEngineers (IIE) and the Institute of Electrical and Electronic Engineers(IEEE) and serves on the editorial board of the International Journal ofIndustrial and Systems Engineering (IJISE).
