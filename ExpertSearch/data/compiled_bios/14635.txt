Audience Tools Search Menu Faculty > Sean Pettersen Sean Pettersen CV Part-Time Lecturer sp72@uw.edu Specializations BI/Data Warehousing Software Development Program Management Courses IMT 576 - Foundations Of Strategic And Managerial Business Intelligence Biography Sean Pettersen is a lecturer at the University of Washington Information School specializing in BI, Data Warehousing, Software Development, and Program Management. He brings over 22 years of enterprise level experience to the classroom and has worked at dev, engineer, architect, program manager, and executive levels. Sean has worked with companies such as T-Mobile, Amazon, Microsoft, NFL, Nike, Blizzard and EA to name a few. In working for these companies, Sean lead large teams in the modernization of the software development capability with a focus on data, automation, and innovation as way to differentiate the organization. He has a Master of Science in Information Management (MSIM) from the University of Washington.
