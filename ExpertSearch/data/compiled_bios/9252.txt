He has been a faculty member of the department since 1995. His research in data-driven storm-surge modeling has had a major impact on the response to natural disasters in Texas, and he was instrumental in establishing the new computational engineering undergraduate program. Dawson holds theJ.J. McKetta Centennial Energy Chair in Engineering.
