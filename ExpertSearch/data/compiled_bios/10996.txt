Faculty Thorsten Kirsch , PhD Professor of Orthopaedic Surgery, Cell Biology, and Biomedical Engineering Biomedical Engineering Connect Vice Chair for Research, Orthopaedic Surgery Director, Musculoskeletal Research Center The ultimate goal of Professor Kirschs research is to define novel therapeutic targets for the treatment of osteoarthritis and other joint diseases and injuries. To this end the laboratory studies mechanisms involved in the regulation of cartilage homeostasis, maintenance and pathology. In addition, research is performed on how cells and the joint environment interact.
