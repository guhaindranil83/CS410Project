Innovation Initiative Research by the Value Chain Innovation Initiative Responsible Supply Chains Responsible Supply Chains There has been growing awareness of the social, environmental, and ethical impacts of todays global supply chains. Our research explores shared value opportunities in which actors along the supply chain can experience economic and social gain.
