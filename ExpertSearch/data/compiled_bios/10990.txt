Faculty Mary Cowman , Ph.D. Professor of Biomedical Engineering and Orthopaedic Surgery Biomedical Engineering Connect Research Interests: Bioanalytical and biophysical chemistry. Structure, function, and medical applications of the biomatrix surrounding cells, with special expertise in hyaluronan polysaccharide. Identification of biomarkers and bioactive fragments derived from extracellular matrix. Development of medical diagnostic assays. Discovery and development of biotherapeutics and biomaterials.
