Demkowicz Professor Assistant Director of the Oden Institute for Computational Engineering and Sciences W.A. "Tex" Moncrief Chair in Computational Engineering & Sciences II Department Research Areas: Computational Engineering Education:Ph.D, D.Sc., Cracow University of Technology, Cracow, Poland Research Interests: Applied Functional Analysis Adaptive Finite and Boundary Element Methods Computational Wave Propagation Computational Solid and Fluid Mechanics Dr. Demkowicz' research combines applied mathematics, continuum mechanics and computational methods for partial differential equations with emphasis on finite element and boundary element methods. He has served alternately on the Cockrell School of Engineering faculties at UT and at Cracow University of Technology since 1980. Dr. Demkowicz has two years of industrial experience and has published six books and more than 200 technical articles, book chapters and reports. From 2012-2019 he served as Editor-in-Chief of Computers and Mathematics with Applications (Elsevier) and continues now as a Managing Editor. He is a fellow of both USACM and IACM and is an honorary member of PACM (US, International and Polish Associations of Mechanics). He served as a president of both PACM and USACM.
