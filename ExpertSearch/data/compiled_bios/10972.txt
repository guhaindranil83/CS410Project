Faculty Alesha Castillo , Ph.D. Assistant Professor of Biomedical Engineering, and Orthopaedic Surgery Biomedical Engineering Connect Dr. Castillo's research focuses on interactions between mechanobiological cues and musculoskeletal systems, with consideration given at the molecular, cellular, and tissue levels.
