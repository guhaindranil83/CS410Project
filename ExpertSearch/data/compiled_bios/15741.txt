Rosen earned his PhD from Princeton (2008), was a Bersoff Fellow in NYUs philosophy department, and spent several years at the Humboldt University in Berlin, first as a research fellow in Excellence Cluster TOPOI, then as Academic Coordinator in the Humboldt Universitys Graduate Program in Ancient Philosophy.
