Prior to coming to Georgia Tech, she received her Ph.D. in Economics from Lehigh University in August 2019. Her research interests include health economics, labor economics, and applied econometrics. Her current research focuses on health information technology, health care policy, occupational regulations, and delivery and usage of health care services.
