Requests will be responded to within 2-3 business days. Classroom Request Form If a request involves any of the following... General public Furniture re-arranging by the Facilites staff Hosted during non-building hours North Creek Events Center Then the room request must go through the Campus Events Office. Facilities Use Approval Committee (FUAC) Request Form A fee may be assessed to your request if it involves additional staff time (Facilities staff moving furniture or cleaning up food/beverage stains, Public Safety patrolling the campus for an event beyond building hours, etc.) Contact Campus Events for more information.
