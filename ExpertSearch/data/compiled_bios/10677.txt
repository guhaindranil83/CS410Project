Hariri Building 595 Commonwealth Avenue Boston, MA 02215 Download CV Tesary Lins research focuses on how information and data moderate the relationship between firms and consumers. She is particularly interested in consumer privacy preferences, the impact of privacy policies, and analytics tools that adapt to the modern information environment. Prior to joining BU Questrom, Tesary worked in Microsoft Research (Chief Economist Office) as well as JD Silicon Valley Lab (Applied Economics Team). She obtained her PhD in quantitative marketing at the University of Chicago. Education PhD, University of Chicago, 2020 Publications Lin, T. (In Press).
