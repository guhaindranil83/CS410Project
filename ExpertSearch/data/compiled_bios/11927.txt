He received his Ph.D. in Industrial Engineering and Operations Research from The Pennsylvania State University. Prior to that, he was a Research Intern at the Department of Mathematics, Penn State University. He received his B.Sc. degree in Mathematics and Applications from The University of Tehran, Tehran, Iran. His research focuses on developing, analyzing, and implementing algorithms to solve optimization problems. His research interest comprises the following interconnected areas: Distributed optimization and Large-scale saddle point problems with applications in machine learning.
