The studio is a resource for teaching, reserach and business liasions in advanced graphics, interaction and visualisation. Its located in the D-building of KTH Main Campus: map and directions. Thuresson has a background in Cinema Studies, Journalism and Communication Studies and professional experience inproduction of film and educational multimedia and in concept development fornew media, as well as software development and IT-management. He has acted as researcher and coordinator in several national and international projects. The current focus is to make the most efficient and strategically and practically sound use of the studio.
