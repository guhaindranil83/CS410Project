Audience Tools Search Menu Faculty > Randal Root Randal Root CV Part-Time Lecturer rootr@uw.edu Specializations Full-Stack Development (Multi-Tiered Web Applications and Services) Business Intelligence Design and Development Database Design and Development Courses INFO 330 - Databases And Data Modeling Biography Randal Root is a senior consultant specializing in application and database development, business intelligence solutions, and technical education. Although he has worked in the industry as a network administrator and programmer since the 1980s, his current focus is teaching in both businesses and schools, including the University of Washington's iSchool. His primary subjects are programming and database development, but he also provides instruction on software testing and network administration. Randal holds several professional certifications including MCSE, MCP+I, MCTS, MCDBA, MCAD, MCT and many others. His published books include A Tester's Guide to .NET Programming (Apress) and Pro SQL 2012 BI Solutions (Apress). He has also received the University of Washington Instructors Excellence Awards in 2012 and 2019.
