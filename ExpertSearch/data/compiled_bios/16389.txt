Henrik is affiliated with the Center for Cyber Defense and Information Security (CDIS) and Secure and Trustworthy Execution Platform (STEP) research group at KTH. He has an MSc in Computer Science and a BSc in Computer Science and Engineering, both from KTH. Henrik's research interests are computer security and formal methods. Henrik's supervisors are Mads Dam (main) and Roberto Guanciale. Currently, Henrik is working on the verification of separation kernels, in particular OpenMZ, a separation kernel for RISC-V.KongligSKis Henrik's research separation kernel and the upstream of OpenMZ.
