Mechanical, Industrial, and Manufacturing Engineering, we are collaborators and communicators, and we take great pride in in our team-based approach to both teaching and research. From hands-on instruction rooted in the real world to cutting-edge research fueled by innovation, our faculty and staff are tackling some of the planet's most pressing problems. Together, our people cut across disciplines and shatter academic silos. We graduate top engineers who know how to work on teams.
