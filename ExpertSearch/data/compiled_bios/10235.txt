Related News Toggle Accordion Biography AboutSally Conant joined Northeastern University as the College of Engineerings Global Co-op Coordinator in 2015. She has her M.A. in International Relations and B.A. in International Studies and Sociology from Salve Regina University. She is currently working towards an MBA from Northeastern. Sally previously coordinated projects and managed daily operations for a large engineering department in a satellite-communications company. She has experience in studying, living, and working abroad in South America, Eastern and Western Europe, and Southeast Asia. Sally is passionate about international travel and co-op and the positive impact it has on students in higher education.EducationM.A. (International Relations) Salve Regina University, 2013B.A. (International Studies & Sociology) Salve Regina University, 2012 Toggle Accordion Related News FacultySep 10, 2020Faculty and Staff Awards 2020Congratulations to all the winners of the faculty and staff awards, and to everyone for their hard work and dedication during the 2019-2020 academic school year.
