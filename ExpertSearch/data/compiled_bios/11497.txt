Lawrence Professor of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at the University of Texas at Arlington is a Life Fellow of ASME. He has both industrial and academic experience and expertise in applied mechanics, finite element methods, vibration and structural dynamics. He is the author/coauthor of three text books related to finite element methods and dynamic systems.
