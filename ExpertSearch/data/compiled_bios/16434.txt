His project is mainly to address ultra-high reliability and reselience for cyber physical systems (CPS). Milad started his work at Ericsson AB in the development unit of radio products as a software developer in 2013 and continued as a technical driver in 2017. He holds M.Sc. in Embedded and Intelligent Systems and B.Sc. in Computer Engineering from Halmstad University and Shiraz University, respectively.
