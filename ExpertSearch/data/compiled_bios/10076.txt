Assistant(s) Research Group Matters Monica Nolasco 107 Steele Laboratory (626) 395-4140 mnolasco@caltech.edu Research Group Website Personal Website Research Overview Research in Richard Murray's group is in the application of feedback and control to networked systems, with applications in biology and autonomy. Current projects include novel control system architectures, biomolecular feedback systems and networked control systems.
