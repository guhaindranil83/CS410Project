Biomedical Engineering, 2008, Politecnico di Milano Ph.D. Materials Engineering, 2011, McGill University Postdoctoral Scholar, 2012-2015, SilkLab, Tufts University ResearchInterestsBenedetto (Ben) Marellis research group works in the area of structural biopolymers, biomineralization and self-assembly. By using biofabrication strategies, the research group designs bio-inspired materials that work at the biotic/abiotic interface toprevent or mitigate environmental impact. His interests and collaborative opportunities span widely across the department. He brings a wealth of experience, and a strong focus onengineering and innovation from a multiple-scale perspective with interests that include mechanical and optoelectronic properties of natural polymers, biocomposites, additive manufacturing, and emerging technologies.
