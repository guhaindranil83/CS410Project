People Research Facts and Figures Faculty The Department of Mechanical Engineering has 37 faculty members, including: 8 assistant professors 13 associate professors 10 professors 3 lecturers 1 senior lecturer 2 research professors 1 adjunct professor 1 joint appointment (primary department not ME) Students The department has an outstanding student body intellectually prepared to handle the rigors of study in an academically challenging major. The majority of our students are Kentucky residents. The department embraces diversity and strives to achieve it within our faculty, staff and student body. Nearly 110 individuals receive their bachelor's degree each year and while most go out into the world to begin their professional experience, many choose to pursue a graduate degree at the University of Kentucky or other highly respected graduate schools.All graduates are well-prepared to meet the challenges of a professional engineering career.
