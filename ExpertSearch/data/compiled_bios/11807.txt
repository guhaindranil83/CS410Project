He received his Bachelors in Mechanical Engineering from NIT Hamirpur. His research interests are largely in the broad area of mechanical behavior of materials with specific focus on (i) understanding the mechanical integrity of structures under multi-physics (mechanical-electromagnetic-thermal) loading, (ii) non-conventional machining at smaller length scale, and (iii) thermal interface materials for next-generation microelectronics packaging applications.
