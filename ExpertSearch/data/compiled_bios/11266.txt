University of St. Andrews, Scotland Courses: ECE 101 Exploring Electrical Engineering ECE 221, 222, 223 Electric Circuit Analysis ECE 321, 322, 323 Electronics ECE 331, 332 Engineering Electromagnetics ECE 424Engineering Professional Practice Profile: Dr. Holtzman is full-time teaching faculty and not currently engaged in research. Before coming to PSU, she taught at Cal Poly San Luis Obispo and University of Nevada Reno, where she also did research in high-speed, frequency-hopping phase-locked loops for telemetry applications. Previously she worked in research and development of infrared sensors at Hughes Aircraft Company and Rockwell International.
