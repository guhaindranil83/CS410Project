He has more than 30 years of experience from both academia and the wireless (radio and radar) industry, where his work has resulted in numerous products, patents and scientific papers. He is the adviser of more than 50 M.Sc., 7 licentiate and 3 PhD thesis and has published 100+ journal articles and conference reports, and served on both ETSI and 3GPP committees. He was KTHs Principal Investigator in theEU (FP7) project on 5G,METIS,and has over the years generated more than $30M for various research projects related to wireless networks, radars, antennas and propagation He is currently a senior researcherin the Radio Systems labanda part time technical expert for Icomera AB while serving as board member of Medfield Diagnostics ABand H&E Solutions AB andtechnical board member of Skysenseand Allgon AB.
