Moussavi, Maryam Rahnavard, Mohammad Raman, Saravana Rezvani, Behruz Rodriguez, Leo Sleiman, Ali Taggart, David Talebi, Mohammad Tran, Duc Zhou, Jun Electrical Engineering Dr. Fei Wang Dr. Fei Wang joined the CSULB Electrical Engineering Department as an assistant professor in 2007 after serving as an assistant professor in the EE department of Cal Poly San Luis Obispo from 2005-2007. She received her MS and Ph.D. degrees in electrical engineering from University of Cincinnati, and her BS degree in electronics and information science from Peking University. Dr. Fei Wang's research interests include non-volatile memory devices, RF and microwave electronics.
