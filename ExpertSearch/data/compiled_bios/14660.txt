Audience Tools Search Menu Faculty > Bree Norlander Bree Norlander CV Data Scientist, Technology & Social Change Group norlab@uw.edu Bloedel Hall Specializations Data Science Data Curation Research Areas Information and Society Data Science Biography Bree is a Research Coordinator & Analyst in the Technology & Social Change (TASCHA) Group at the University of Washington (UW) Information School. She received her M.L.I.S. from the UW iSchool specializing in Data Science and Data Curation. She also works as a project manager for the UW iSchools Open Data Literacy program. Her research focuses on curating Open Data and analyzing Open Data in new and creative ways to advance and expand scientific scholarship.
