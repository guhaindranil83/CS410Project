Jeremy teaches courses on design thinking, information architecture, and cooperative software development at the UW iSchool, along with courses on front-end development at UW PCE.In addition, Jeremy enjoys mentoring entrepreneurs and helping grow the local startup ecosystem. He is Managing Director of Founder Institute Seattle, a Venture Partner at Loyal VC, and a mentor at Galvanize and UW.
