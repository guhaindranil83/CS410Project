He has been lecturer and then senior lecturer in philosophy at the University of Bristol (1998-2011) and long-term Visitor at the Institute for Advanced Study, Princeton (2011-2015). He is interested in the history of philosophy, especially Plato, and in certain issues in the philosophy of action and mind. In 2018, he published No Morality, No Self, on G. E. M. Anscombe's papers 'Modern Moral Philosophy' and 'The First Person'. He is working on books on Plato's Gorgias, Augustine's Confessions and Anscombe's Intention.
