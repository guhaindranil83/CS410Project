Faculty Manpreet Katari , Ph.D. Clinical Associate Professor Connect Manpreet Katari is a Clinical Associate Professor in the Department of Biology in the College of Arts and Science. He teaches undergraduate and graduate level courses focusing on Bioinformatics related subjects. Courses range from Statistics, Applied Genomics, Transcriptomics, Database and Datamining, and Fundamental Bioinformatics. Manpreet's research interest is in developing tools to analyze next-generation sequence and systems biology data.
