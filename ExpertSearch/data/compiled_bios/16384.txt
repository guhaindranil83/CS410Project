I defended my PhD thesis in July 2018 that you can readhere. My main topics of interest are high-speed networking, network function virtualization, operating systems and high-performance programming. I'm the main author of FastClick, an opensource very high-performance packet processing framework, and continue to maintain it on a daily basis. It is being used by multiple researchers to prototype NFV systems. Since then, I worked on enhancing stateful service chaining by factorizing common work among VNFs.
