InstitutionsMacroeconomicsFinancial MarketsBio Juliane Begenau is an Assistant Professor of Finance at the Stanford Graduate School of Business. Prior to that, she taught at Harvard Business School. She is also a Faculty Research Fellow at the National Bureau of Economic Research and a Research Affiliate at CEPR. Her research focus is the interplay of the real economy with financial markets and financial institutions. Begenau earned her Ph.D. in economics at Stanford University.
