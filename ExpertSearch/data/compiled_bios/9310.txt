is a meteorologist, who teaches large-scale dynamics and synoptic meteorology to graduate and undergraduate students. She is responsible for the Synoptic Laboratory. Her research interests are in synoptic meteorology, severe weather and atmospheric blocking. She is also involved in developing innovative teaching methods and outreach to the public and schools - see Weather in a Tank. Publications Illari, L., Marshall, J., and W. D.
