Current Student Transfer Student Faculty / Staff Student Veteran Parent Alumni Vikings Fan PSU Electrical and Computer Engineering Richard Tymerski Richard Tymerski Associate Professor Contact: Fourth Avenue Building 160-18 (503)725-5424h8rt@pdx.eduFaculty Website:http://web.cecs.pdx.edu/~tymerski/ Education: Ph.D. Virginia Polytechnic Institute and State University M.S.California Institute of Technology M.Eng.Sc.University of New South Wales, AustraliaResearch Interests: Power Electronics Courses: ECE 101Exploring Electrical Engineering ECE 222Electronic Circuit Analysis II ECE 241Introduction to Electrical Engineering ECE 331Engineering Electromagnetics I ECE410/510CFEmerging Functional Verification Methods ECE 451/551Control Systems Design I ECE 452/552Control Systems Design II ECE445/545Power Electronic Systems Design I ECE446/546Power Electronic Design II Profile: Richard Tymerski (S87M88) received his B.Sc. degree in mathematics and his B.E. and M.Eng.Sc. degrees in electrical engineering from the University of New South Wales, Sydney, Australia, in 1977, 1980, and 1983, respectively, his M.S. degree from California Institute of Technology, Pasadena, in 1984, and his Ph.D. degree from Virginia Polytechnic Institute and State University, Blacksburg, in 1988, both in electrical engineering. During 19801983, he was an Electronics Engineer with Medtel Pty. Ltd. and Newsound Electronics Pty. Ltd., Sydney, Australia. Since 1988, he has been with the Electrical Engineering Department, Portland State University, where he is currently an Associate Professor. During the 19981999 academic year, he was a Japan Society for the Promotion of Science Fellow at Kyushu University, Fukuoka, Japan.
