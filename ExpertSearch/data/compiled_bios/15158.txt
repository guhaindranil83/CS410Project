Wright St. M/C 374 Champaign, IL 61820 xinranli@illinois.edu Edit Your Profile Assistant Professor BiographyI am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Statistics at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. Prior to this, I spent a year as a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Statistics at the University of Pennsylvania. I obtained my Ph.D. in Statistics from Harvard University in 2018, under the supervision of Jun S. Liu and Donald B. Rubin.
