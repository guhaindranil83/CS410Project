She was a recipient of Thomas Rumble Fellowship while pursuing Ph.D. She did MS in Computer Engineering from Wayne State University in 1985. She was awarded commonwealth scholarship to do M.Sc in Medical Physics at the University of Aberdeen, Scotland, UK. She was a recipient of Fulbright Senior Specialist award in 2003 and visited Nippon Institute of Technology and Tokyo Denki University in Japan. She is a senior member of IEEE. She is a reviewer for IEEE, WSEAS and other international journals and conferences. She is a reviewer of NSF and served on multiple NSF panels. Dr. Kaurs research was funded by University of Toledo, Daimler Chrysler, AFOSOR, NSF and AFRL. Recent Publications Blen.
