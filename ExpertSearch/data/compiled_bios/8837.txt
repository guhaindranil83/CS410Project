Industrial Organization Seminars Find seminars on industrial organization that are open to Stanford GSB faculty and PhD students, or request permission to attend. IO seminars are held on Tuesdays from 12:00 to 1:15 p.m., unless otherwise indicated. Stanford GSB faculty may also schedule a session to meet the speaker individually. The list also includes the (IO)2 seminar series, which are also scheduled for Tuesdays from 12:00 to 1:15 p.m., every other Tuesday than the internal seminar.
