540-231-1616 Research expertise Biomechanics Dynamics and control Fluid Mechanics Solid Mechanics The Ross Dynamics Lab at Virginia Tech specializes in applications of nonlinear dynamics, performing mathematical modeling, simulation, visualization, and experiments with applications in several different fields, including: Patterns of dispersal in oceanic and atmospheric flows Passive and active aerodynamic gliding Dynamic buckling of flexible structures Transport across the air-water interface Orbital mechanics Chemical physics Causality analysis in complex natural and artificial systems
