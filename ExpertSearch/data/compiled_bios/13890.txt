He received the B.Sc. degree, with Honour, in Electrical Power Engineering from Ain Shams University, Cairo, Egypt, in 2007. Dr. Radwan is currently an Assistant Professor at Western Washington University, Bellingham, WA, USA. He has published manyjournal papers andinternational conferences in the areas of renewable energy integration, smart active distribution systems, and control of power electronic converters. Dr. Radwanis IEEESenior Memberand isa licensedProfessional Engineer in Saskatchewan, Canada.
