Since 2016, he is an Assistant Professor at the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department and a fellow at the Grainger Institute for Engineering at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. His research interests include information theory, machine learning, and network science. He is a recipient of the NSF-CAREER Award (2020), the Eli Jury Award from the EECS Department at UC Berkeley (2015), and the Jack Keil Wolf student paper award at ISIT 2015.
