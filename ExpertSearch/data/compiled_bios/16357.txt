After working at BBN Technologies as a data analyst, he obtained his Ph.D. in Computer Science at Johns Hopkins University. Prior to joining UW-Madison, he was a postdoctoral research fellow in the Department of Statistics at University of Michigan. In his spare time, Keith enjoys cooking, hiking and playing jazz bass.
