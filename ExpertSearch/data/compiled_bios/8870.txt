The Eddie Lunch is a weekly brown bag lunch open to Stanford and visiting faculty. This lunch was founded and run for many years by our late colleague Eddie Lazear. The meetings are held Thursdays from 12:00 to 1:00 p.m., unless otherwise indicated.
