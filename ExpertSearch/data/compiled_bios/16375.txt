School of Informatics, Computing, and Engineering features world-renowned faculty who are innovative leaders in their respective fields.With unsurpassed breadth and more than 3,000 graduate and undergraduate students, Luddy is among the largest schools of its kind in the country and offers degrees in computer science, informatics, intelligent systems engineering, data science, and
