I am interested in fraud and misreporting in public expenditures, particularly in the Medicare and Medicaid programs, as well as the consequences of fraud for public spending and patient health outcomes. I am also interested in the detection and deterrence of fraud and corruption, as well as the statistical properties of misreported data. Please feel free to email me if you would like to chat about teaching, research, or my outreach work on diversity and inclusion.
