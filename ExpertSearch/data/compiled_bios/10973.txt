Faculty Joel S. Schuman , MD, FACS Elaine Langone Prof. of Ophthalmology Prof. of Neuroscience and Physiology Prof. of Biomedical Engineering Prof of Electrical and Computer Engineering Biomedical Engineering Electrical and Computer Engineering Connect Vice Chair for Research,Dept. of Ophthalmology Director, NYU Langone Eye Center ProfessorSchuman leads the Advanced Ophthalmic Imaging Laboratory (AOIL), which consists of a team of ophthalmologists, engineers, software specialists, statisticians, and medical and engineering trainees. He and his colleagues focus primarily on the study of glaucoma, a leading cause of blindness in the world. They develop advanced imaging tools to diagnose this debilitating disease, monitor its progression, and investigate its pathogenesis.
