Prior to joining NYU Shanghai, he was appointed as an Associate Professor at the University of Oxford from 2005 to 2014.Tarrsalso serves as a Co-Director of theNYU-ECNU Institute of Mathematical Sciences at NYU Shanghai.Prof. Tarrs is an expert on self-interacting random processes, particularly reinforced random walks, and their relationship with stochastic algorithms and learning processes in game theory.
