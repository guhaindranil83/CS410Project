Research Prof. Du's primary research interests are surface chemical processes and surface modification, processing and measurements of electronic and photonic materials for integrated devices, surface-enhanced Raman spectroscopy for chemical and biological sensing and biomedical imagingHis current research activities include high-temperature environmental stability of engineering ceramics, fabrication of photonic crystal fibers and long-period fiber gratings, molecular- and nano- scale functionalization of photonic crystal fibers for ultra-sensitive detection and molecular fingerprinting.
