My projects highlight three main aspects of my research the mathematical modeling of the interactions of light with materials and the atmosphere; the design of novel cameras and programmable lighting; and the development of algorithms for rendering and interpreting scene appearance. My research is motivated by applications in a wide range of fields including robotics, intelligent transportation, digital entertainment, remote sensing, underwater imaging and medical imaging.
