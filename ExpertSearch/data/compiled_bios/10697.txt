Hariri Building 595 Commonwealth Avenue Boston, MA 02215 Download CV Leroy Gonsalves is an Assistant Professor of Management and Organizations in the Questrom School of Business at Boston University. He holds a Ph.D. in Organizational Behavior from Harvard Business School. Professor Gonsalves research examines the consequences of employer practices with a focus on diversity, work-life issues, and social responsibility. His work appears in Administrative Science Quarterly.
