Biography Biography Prof. Luca Maddalena is a Full Professor of Aerospace Engineering and the Director of the Aerodynamics Research Center, ARC.He is responsible for research operations in its low-speed wind tunnel, transonic Ludwieg tube tunnel (0.4His research has been funded by NASA Langley Research Center (Hypervelocity Propulsion, Mach 8+), the Office of Naval Research (ONR) for his contribution to the Strike WEApon Program (WEAP), the Air Force Research Laboratory on the Next Generation Thermal Protection Systems Program, the Defense Advanced Research Projects Agency (DARPA) and the Department of Energy (DOE). ONR and DARPA have jointly funded Dr. Maddalena's group for the design and the construction of a National-Level Hypersonic Research Facility of High-Temperature Materials Development and Characterization.Prof. Maddalena is a Fellow of the Royal Aeronautical Society and an Associate Fellow of the American Institute of Aeronautics and Astronautics (AIAA). He presently serves as Deputy Director for Aircraft Technologies within the AIAA's Aircraft Technology, Integration and Operations (ATIO) Group. Dr. Maddalena has served as Chair of the AIAA's Hypersonic Technologies and Aerospace Planes (HyTASP) Technical Committee from 2017 to 2019 and is a member of the HyTASP Steering Committee.
