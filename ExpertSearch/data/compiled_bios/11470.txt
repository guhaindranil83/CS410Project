Eligible applicants must be in the early stages of their careers, have received a PhD and hold a tenure-track assistant professor position at an accredited university. These prestigious awards denote scientific excellence and innovation among emerging researchers, indicating the nations future leaders in scientific research. NSF Presidential Young Investigator Award (1984-1991) The Presidential Young Investigator Award was a precursor of the NSF CAREER award. It was granted to junior faculty within three years of their hire as assistant professors at accredited universities in recognition of their research promise in the sciences and engineering. C.H. Menq Giorgio Rizzoni Vish Subramaniam NSF Faculty Early Career Development (CAREER) Program The CAREER Award is granted by the National Science Foundation to outstanding early career faculty who serve as leaders and role models in the integration of research and education. Lian Duan Mrinal Kumar Jack McNamara Jeffrey Sutton ARO Young Investigator Award The Army Research Office Young Investigator Award is granted to outstanding early career faculty for key research in science and engineering on behalf of the Army. James Gregory ASME Haythornthwaite Young Investigator Award The American Society of Mechanical Engineers Haythornthwaite Young Investigator Award is granted to outstanding early career faculty for key research in in theoretical and applied mechanics. Ryan Harne Ruike Zhao DARPA Young Faculty Award The Defense Advanced Research Projects Agency Young Faculty Award is granted to outstanding faculty in junior research positions to fund research in areas of national security. Hanna Cho Defense Threat Reduction Agency (DTRA) Young Investigator Award Raymond Cao NASA Early Career Faculty Award The NASA Early Career Faculty Award is granted to outstanding early career faculty for key research in areas of science, space travel and exploration that is effective, sustainable and affordable. Ran Dai ONR Young Investigator Award The Office of Naval Research Young Investigator Award is granted to outstanding early career faculty for key research to aid the Department of the Navys Science and Technology. Presidential Early Career Awards for Scientists and Engineers (PECASE) The PECASE Award is granted to recent recipients of the CAREER Award on the basis of two criteria: 1) innovative research relevant to the mission of the NSF, and 2) community service demonstrated through scientific leadership, education or community outreach.
