She examines how the presence of advanced technology in the marketplace impacts consumers responses and behaviors. Ilanas research has been published in the Journal of Marketing Research, the Journal of Consumer Affairs, and the Journal of Purchasing and Supply Management. Before pursuing a doctoral degree, Ilana worked in ultra-high net worth private wealth management for twelve years, including five years at UBS Financial Services and seven years at Merrill Lynch.
