UC Berkeley - Department of Philosophy Sara Magrin Toggle navigation Department Faculty Advising Sara Magrin [home page] Associate Professor of Classics Office: 7216 Dwinelle E-mail: magrin_s@berkeley.edu (PhD McGill, Philosophy). She is a faculty member in the department of classics, and she specializes in ancient philosophy. Her research focuses on ancient epistemology and psychology, and she has published on ancient skepticism and on Plotinus. She is working on a book tentatively titled With the Whole Soul: Plotinus on Animal and Human Forms of Cognition and Desire.
