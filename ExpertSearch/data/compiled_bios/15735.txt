She currently works on topics in the philosophy of mind and epistemology. Her books The Contents of Visual Experience (2010) and The Rationality of Perception (2017) were both published by Oxford University Press. Other publications include: Inference without Reckoning. Oxford University Press. 2015. Cognitive Penetrability: Modularity, Epistemology, and Ethics with Zoe Jenkin. Review of Psychology and Philosophy. 2015.
