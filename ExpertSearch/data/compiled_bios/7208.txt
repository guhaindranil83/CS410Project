In 2006, Dr. Bloch received the Ph.D. degree in Engineering Science from the Universit de Franche-Comt, France, and in 2008, he received the Ph.D. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Georgia Tech. He spent a year as a postdoctoral research associate at the University of Notre Dame and joined the faculty at Georgia Tech in July 2009. At the start of his career, Dr. Bloch was based at the Georgia Tech Lorraine campus in Metz, France, and in 2013, he moved to the main Georgia Tech campus in Atlanta.
