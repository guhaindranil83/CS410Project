Or just stop by. Degrees Ph.D in Mathematics from Johns Hopkins University Bio Mike Limarzi earned his Ph.D. from Johns Hopkins University in 2011 focusing in algebraic number theory. He received his bachelor's degree from Georgetown University, spending a semester abroad in Florence, Italy. He has taught a variety of mathematics and statistics courses at Johns Hopkins University and Towson University. The co-founder of Charm City Tutors, he is passionate about sharing his love of mathematics with all students.
