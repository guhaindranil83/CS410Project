People Husam Quteineh People Husam Quteineh Postgraduate Research Student School of Computer Science and Electronic Engineering Senior Research Officer School of Computer Science and Electronic Engineering (CSEE) Email husam.quteineh@essex.ac.uk Location Colchester Campus Profile Contact Profile Contact Profile Biography I am a Senior Research Officer at the Business and Local Government (BLG) Data Research Centre. I'm also a PhD student in Computer Science. My PhD project is focused on Training Neural Network classifiers with insufficient textual data. My main research interests include Natural Language Processing and Neural Networks. I also have interests in building NLP applications including chatbots and question answering systems. Qualifications PhD University of Essex, MSc in Big Data and Text Analytics University of Essex, Bachelors of Science in Computer Science American University of Beirut, Appointments Other academic Senior Research Officer, University of Essex (2019 - present) Contact husam.quteineh@essex.ac.uk Location: Colchester Campus WE ARE ESSEX ARE YOU?
