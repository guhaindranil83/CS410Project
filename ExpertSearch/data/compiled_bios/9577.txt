Research interests predominantly relate to geophysical site characterization, nondestructive evaluation and rehabilitation of foundations, bridge scour, and urban seismic hazards, particularly as related to resiliency of infrastructure systems. Educational interests focus on improving student cognitive development and critical thinking skills within the context of geotechnical engineering coursework.
