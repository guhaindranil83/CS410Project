Stanford GSB You are hereThe Stanford GSB Experience Learning at Stanford GSB Our Faculty Our Faculty Our faculty are recognized experts in the global business community. Theyre knowledge creators who rigorously pursue research at the leading edge of their fields. And theyre passionate about developing the next generation of global business leaders. Stanford GSB has 111 faculty featuring Nobel laureates, members of the National Academy of Sciences, and members of the American Academy of Arts and Sciences. During your time at Stanford GSB, youll have direct access to their expertise and insight. Youll form close-knit relationships with professors who will challenge you and inspire you to expand your knowledge, follow your passion, and pursue opportunities that will create impact.
