Cao is a graduate of The Ohio State University.His research focuses on the areas of radar signal processing, electronically scanned radar systems, radar imaging and machine learning with an emphasis on radar applications. Degrees PhD: electrical and computer engineering, The Ohio State University, 2014 MS: electrical and computer engineering, The Ohio State University, 2014 MS: electronics and information, South China University of Technology, 2010 BS: telecommunications engineering, Xidian University, 2007 Teaching Interests Radar systems, signal processingand random processing. Research Interests Radar signal processing, electronically scanned radar systems, radar imaging and machine learning with an emphasis on radar applications.
