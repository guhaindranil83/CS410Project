Cornell University, 1996 M.Sc. Princeton University, 1998 Ph.D. Cambridge University, 2002 Research InterestsProfessor Ochsendorf conducts research on the mechanics and behavior of historical structures, with a primary focus on the collapse of masonry structures. His group is actively researching the dynamics of masonry buildings, the safety of cracked masonry vaults and domes, displacement loading of structures and the design of more sustainable infrastructure.
