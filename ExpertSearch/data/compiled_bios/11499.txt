His experience includes ten years as full time aerospace engineering faculty as well as ten years in the industry, including ten years of engineering research entrepreneurship as founder and manager of two small businesses focused on innovative engineering solutions in the areas of inflatable structures and airship technology from concept to modeling, design and testing, and practical implementation. Dr. Dancila has taught courses at both the undergraduate and the graduate level, and has advised undergraduate and graduate students, leading to the awarding of three Ph.D. and nine M.S. degrees. He has authored fourteen refereed and over forty conference publications, and has been awarded four U.S patents. Dr. Dancila has experience as a private pilot with glider and FAA Single Engine Land ratings. Education Education 1998 PhD in Aerospace Eng.
