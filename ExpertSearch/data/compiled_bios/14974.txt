In geometry, Dr. Helliwell studies geometric flow, which can be thought of as understanding the way surfaces and their higher-dimensional analogs evolve according to their own curvature. Additionally, Dr. Helliwell enjoys exploring questions in taxicab geometry with undergraduate researchers. Finally, Dr. Helliwell is involved in projects in analysis exploring the analytical properties of various types of domains. Recent Publications Click here for Dr. Helliwells publications Biography Dr. Helliwell has a B.S. in Mathematics from Harvey Mudd College and a Ph.D. in Mathematics from the University of Washington (2005). He has been on faculty at Seattle University since 2005.
