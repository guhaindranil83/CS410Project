Dr. Lemanski is a Licensed Specialist in School Psychology (LSSP) in the state of Texas. She received her doctorate in Developmental Psychology from Pennsylvania State University and was later recertified as a School Psychology at the University of Houston-Clear Lake. She has extensive experience in the school setting, working in several school districts across the Houston area, most recently Cypress-Fairbanks Independent School District. She also previously acted as an Adjunct Lecturer at the University of Houston Main Campus. Dr. Lemanski sits on the Professional Advisory Committee for the Attention Deficit Disorders Association - Southern Region (ADDA-SR). Dr. Lemanskis areas of research interests include: The Development of Romantic Relationships, High School, College, Transition, and Career Planning, the Use Support Groups, and the Development of Self Advocacy Skills in Adolescents with ADHD and/or High Functioning Autism. Selected Presentation: Lemanski, M.R., Guillen, M., and Pool, C. (October 2020). The Role of the LSSP in Transitioning Special Education Students to High School & Beyond! Mini Skills Session to be presented at the TASP 2020 Conference, San Antonio, TX.
