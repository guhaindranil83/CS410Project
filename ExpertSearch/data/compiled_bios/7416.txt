She is also a member of the Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence Laboratory (CSAIL) and MachineLearning@MIT. Before moving to MIT, she completed her PhD at UC Berkeley with Michael I. Jordan. Prof. Broderick works in the areas of machine learning andstatistics. She is particularly interested in Bayesian statistics and graphical modelswith an emphasis on scalable, nonparametric, and unsupervised learning.
