Jaakkola received his MSc in theoretical physics from Helsinki University of Technology, 1992, and PhD from MIT in computational neuroscience, 1997. Following a postdoctoral position in computational molecular biology (DOE/Sloan fellow, UCSC) he joined the MIT EECS faculty 1998. His applied research focuses on problems in computational functional genomics such as transcriptional regulation.
