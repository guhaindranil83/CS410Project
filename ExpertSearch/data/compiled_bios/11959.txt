Long [home page] Irving Stone Professor of Literature Emeritus (Department of Classics) E-mail: aalong@berkeley.edu (Ph.D., University of London). His main field of teaching and research is ancient philosophy. He is editor of The Cambridge Companion to Early Greek Philosophy and his other books include Hellenistic Philosophy (University of California Press) and Epictetus: A Stoic and Socratic Guide to Life (Oxford University Press).
