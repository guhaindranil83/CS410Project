I-HUNG KHOO Dr. Khoo joined CSULB in 2006. He holds a joint appointment in the Electrical Engineering and Biomedical Engineering departments. Dr. Khoo's research interests include high-speed and mixed-signal circuit design, and analog and digital signal processing. He is currently the advisor for the Electronics and Computer Engineering Technology programs. Dr. Khoo is a member of IEEE and received his Ph.D. in electrical and computer engineering from University of California, Irvine in 2002.
