Faculty Samir Taneja , MD Professor of Urology, Radiology, and Biomedical Engineering Biomedical Engineering Connect Vice Chair, Urology Director, Division of Urologic Oncology GU Program Director, Perlmutter Cancer Center As director of the Smilow Comprehensive Prostate Cancer Center (SCPCC),Prof. Samir Taneja is conducting research that focuses on the use of MRI to improve methods of prostate imaging, cancer detection, disease localization, and treatment. The work of his team hastransformed the field of prostate cancer diagnosis and therapy over the last 15 years. Research Interests: urologic oncology, prostate cancer imaging, diagnosis, treatment, magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) Education MD from Northwestern University, 1990 Residency, UCLA Medical Center, 1992 Residency, UCLA Medical Center, 1996 Experience James M.
