Biography Biography Amir Ameri joined the College in spring 2019 as a senior lecturer and was hired to a tenure-track position in fall 2019. He earned his doctoral degree from the University of Toledo in 2018. Prior to UTA, he was an additive manufacturing design/application engineer at Voestalpine in Houston. He also worked as a design engineer in Iran. Ameri has authored or co-authored 24 journal publications, 13 conference publications and two book chapters. He is a member of SME, ASME, ASB and ORS, serves on the editorial boards of 5 journals, and is a reviewer for 19 journals.
