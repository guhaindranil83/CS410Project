Green St. Urbana, IL 61801 (217) 244-4207 wongt@illinois.edu Research AreasGalaxies and ISMObservation Edit Your Profile Professor View CV Research InterestsMolecular CloudsStar Formation in Nearby GalaxiesEvolution of Disk Galaxies Research DescriptionProf. Wong's research group uses observations of interstellar gas in galaxies to understand their structure and evolution. We study primarily CO, an abundant tracer of molecular gas in galaxies, and investigate the links between molecular clouds, their surrounding galaxies, and the rate of star formation. We also use gas as a probe of the dynamics of galaxy disks.
