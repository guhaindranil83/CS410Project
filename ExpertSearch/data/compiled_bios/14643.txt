Schildkraut is an Assistant Teaching Professor at the University of Washington Information School specializing in management of information systems, project management, and communication and professionalism. Her work focuses on giving young people tools to achieve IT career success and helping organizations create environments that foster success for young workers. Laura has an MBA in Business from New York University and a BS in Business Administration from the State University of New York at Albany.
