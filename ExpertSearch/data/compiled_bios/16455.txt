He obtained his PhD from Trinity College Dublin in 2004 and is a member of the SRA ICT-The Next Generation, aplatform for the recruitment of talented faculty at KTH. His research interests include the investigation of interrelationships between computational visual attention, affect, memory, theory of mind and gaze control models for real-time control, perception of, and multimodal interaction with humanoid agents, groups and crowds in urban and other spatial contexts. These works cross the domains of virtual characters, physical social/mobile robots and computer graphics/virtual reality. To this end, he has participated in numerous national andinternational projects, includingthe EUFP6Network of Excellence HUMAINE(4.95M), EU FP6 Focused Research Project CALLAS(9.5M)and Science Foundation Ireland (SFI) funded METROPOLIS (2.5M). He was the PI for KTH in the EUHorizon 2020 Innovation project ProsocialLearn(4.2M), and is the PI for KTH in the ongoing EU Horizon 2020 MSCA Innovative Training Networks ANIMATAS (3.9M) and CLIPE (4M). Activities Deep-learning in virtual reality for effective human-robot group collaboration Principal Investigator for a four year, 4 MSEK (~380k) Swedish Research Council (Vetenskapsrdet) project on applications of virtual reality to designing and evaluating aspects of real world interactions. CLIPE Principal Investigator for KTH with Jonas Beskow and secondment coordinator in the four year 4M EU Horizon 2020 Innovative Training Network CLIPE: Creative Lively Interactive Populated Environments. Climate smart planning with digital transportation performance certificates Co-Principal Investigator on a three year, 4.5 MSEK (~430k) Swedish Energy Agency project with Todor Stojanovski and Rebakah Cupitt on digital transport research and tools related to procedural content generation and virtual reality. ANIMATAS Principal Investigator for KTH and training coordinator in the four year 3.9m EU Horizon 2020 Innovative Training Network ANIMATAS:Advancing intuitive human-machine interaction with human-like social capabilities for education in schools. IVA 2017 General co-chair, Intelligent Virtual Agents (IVA) conference that took place at KTH, Stockholm in August 2017 after taking place the previous year at the University of Southern California's Institute for Creative Technologies. VIRTUALROBOT Co-Principal Investigator on a three year, 4.5MSEK (~430k) SRA ICT TNG project with Gabriel Skantze exploring situated interaction with social robots using mixed reality. Principal Investigator for KTH in the three year 4.2m EU Horizon 2020 Innovation project ProsocialLearn, concerning prosocial scenarios involving virtual characters for positive learning experiences using computer game technologies. STEAM Co-Principal Investigator with Johan Hoffman on 118 KSEK pedagogy grant on the application of computer game technologies and activity-led learning for engaging computer science students in mathematics, programming, numerical methods and simulation. SIGRAD 2015 General co-chair, SIGRAD (Eurographics Swedish chapter) conference on computer graphics and animation.
