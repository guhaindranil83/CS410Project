Current Student Transfer Student Faculty / Staff Student Veteran Parent Alumni Vikings Fan PSU Electrical and Computer Engineering Fu Li Fu Li Professor Contact: Fourth Avenue Building 160-10 (503) 725-3824 lif@pdx.edu Faculty Website:http://web.cecs.pdx.edu/~fli/ Education: Ph.D. University of Rhode Island M.S.Sichuan University (China) B.S.Sichuan University (China) Research Interests: Signal processing: array, image, and video Communications: mobile and wireless technologies Computer vision Courses: ECE 419/519 Linear Systems ECE 461/561 Communication Systems I ECE 462/562 Communication Systems II Profile: Fu Li received his B.S. and M.S. degrees in physics from Sichuan University, Chengdu, Sichuan, China, in 1982 and 1985, respectively, and his Ph.D. degree in electrical engineering from the University of Rhode Island, Kingston, in 1990. From 1989 to 1990, he was with Prime Computer, Inc., Bedford, MA, as a member of the technical staff. Since 1990, he has been with the Portland State University and is a Full Professor of Electrical Engineering.
