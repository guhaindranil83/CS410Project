Bennighof Research Professor Education:Ph.D., Virginia Polytechnic Institute and University Research Interests: Computation in Structural Dynamics Noise and Vibration Analysis of Vehicles Minimum Time Control of Structures Dr. Bennighof's research focuses on computation and modeling of structural dynamics and on control of flexible structures. He has served on the Cockrell School of Engineering faculty since 1986 and has published more than 30 technical articles and reports.
