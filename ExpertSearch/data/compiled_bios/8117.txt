Applications come from many different fields including Computer Vision, Semiconductors, and Game Theory. One major application is improved understanding of thin-film semiconductor devices. I am currently working on a Department of Energy project on II-VI semiconductor materials joint with industrial collaborators and an interdisciplinary team at Arizona State University.
