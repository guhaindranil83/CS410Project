She is also affiliated with the Program in Critical Theory at Berkeley. Her research is in 19th and 20th century European philosophy, with a focus on the work of G.W.F. Hegel. She is the author of Hegel on Second Nature in Ethical Life (Cambridge University Press, 2017) and is currently working a project about Hegels concept of experience. She has additional interests in Nietzsche, critical theory, and feminist epistemology.
