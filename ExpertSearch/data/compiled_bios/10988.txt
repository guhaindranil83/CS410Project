Faculty Ivan Selesnick Department Chair and Professor of Electrical and Computer Engineering Professor of Biomedical Engineering, and Radiology Biomedical Engineering Electrical and Computer Engineering Connect Ivan Selesnick is a Professor of Electrical and Computer Engineering at NYU Tandon School of Engineering. He received the BS, MEE, and PhD degrees in Electrical Engineering from Rice University in Houston, Texas. He joined Polytechnic University in 1997 (now NYU Tandon School of Engineering). He received an Alexander von Humboldt Fellowship in 1997 and a National Science Foundation Career award in 1999. In 2003, he received the Jacobs Excellence in Education Award from Polytechnic University. He became an IEEE Fellow in 2016. His research interests are in signal and image processing, wavelet-based signal processing, sparsity techniques, and biomedical signal processing. He has been an associate editor for the IEEE Transactions on Image Processing, IEEE Signal Processing Letters, IEEE Transactions on Signal Processing, and IEEE Transactions on Computational Imaging.
