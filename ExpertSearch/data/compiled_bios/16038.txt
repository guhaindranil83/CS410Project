Industry InsightsMake the Future podcast Staff and professor directory COVID-19: Last Update Qingsheng Zeng E-Card Qingsheng ZengAdjunct ProfessorPh.D.Office: 613 990-4920Work E-mail: qzeng@uOttawa.ca Biography Qingsheng ZENG received his Ph.D. from University of Ottawa, Canada. He is an adjunct professor in School of EECS at University of Ottawa, and has co-supervised postdoctoral fellows and PhD students. Dr. Zeng has served as an editorial board member and a reviewer for a number of technical books and scientific journals, as a session organizer and chair, a technical program committee member and reviewer, a short course/workshop/tutorial presenter and a keynote speaker for numerous international symposiums, and as the Chair of AP (Antennas and Propagation) / MTT (Microwave Theory and Techniques) Joint Chapter of IEEE Ottawa Section. He is a senior member of IEEE. He has authored and co-authored one book, two book chapters and more than 60 technical papers and reports in these fields. Research Interests ultra wideband technology numerical electromagnetics electromagnetic compatibility and interference radio wave propagation antennas analysis and design noise modeling and measurement.
