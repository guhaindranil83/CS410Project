Allen Wallis Professor of Econometrics and Statistics Emeritus Email: george.tiao@chicagobooth.edu Address: 5807 South Woodlawn AvenueChicago, IL 60637 Biography Member of the faculty since 1982. A.B. (economics), National Taiwan University, 1955; MBA (banking and finance), New York University, 1958; PhD (economics), University of Wisconsin, Madison, 1962. Research in Bayesian inference; econometric methods; time series methods and applications; modeling and analysis of air pollution and stratospheric ozone data.
