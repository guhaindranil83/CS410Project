After his PhD in 2001 from North Carolina State, he was a director-funded post-doc fellow at Los Alamos National Lab until joining Texas A & M University 2005. Professor Zhang's research interests are in nanostructured metallic systems (thin films and bulk); he couples materials processing with characterization and testing on a wide range of nanostructured metals in both ambient and harsh environments. Education Ph.D., Materials Science & Engineering, North Carolina State University, Raleigh, North Carolina, 2001 M.
