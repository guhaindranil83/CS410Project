My research interests span the areas of real-time systems, programming languages and compilers. After havingreceivedmy bachelors degree in the year 2010, I worked in the industry for 2 yearscontributing to thedevelopment of the3G technology. In the year 2012, I decided to pursue research and enrolled myself for M.Sc. Engg, a research program at Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore. During my masters, I did my research in the area of real-time system, scheduling algorithm, operating system and multicore technology. Publications 1. S. Natarajan, M. Nasri, D. Broman, B.
