Prior to joining the Department of Animal Sciences as Chair in 2019, he served 14 years as the Director of the Range Cattle Research and Education Center in Ona, FL. Academic Positions Since Final Degree: 2019-Present;Professor and Chair, Department of Animal Sciences, University of Florida, Institute of Food and Agricultural Sciences (IFAS) 2009-2019;Professor and Director, Range Cattle Research and Education Center, University of Florida, Institute of Food and Agricultural Sciences (IFAS) Professor (2009), Associate Professor (2003), and Assistant Professor (1998), Range Cattle Research and Education Center and Department of Animal Sciences, University of Florida, IFAS, Ona, FL. 1995 to 1998; Technical Research Scientist, American Protein Corporation, Ames, IA. 1991 to 1995; Graduate Research Assistant, Department of Animal Sciences and Department of Anatomy and Physiology, Kansas State University, Manhattan, KS. Research Faculty appointment divided among the Florida Cooperative Extension Service and the Florida Agricultural Experiment Station. Research and Education programs focus on, (1) the trace mineral nutrition of grazing beef cattle with a specific emphasis on management system aimed at ensuring adequate Cu and Se nutrition of the peri-weaned calf, and (2) relationships among production stress and subsequent immune competence, well-being and productivity of beef cattle. These models have specifically focused on inflammatory reactions and stress tolerances to normal cattle management practices including weaning, commingling, and transportation.
