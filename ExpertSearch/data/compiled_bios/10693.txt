Hariri Building 595 Commonwealth Avenue Boston, MA 02215 Download CV Chiara Longoni is Assistant Professor of Marketing at Boston Universitys Questrom School of Business. Her research explores the social impact of artificial intelligence, technology, and disruptive innovations, as well as a number of antecedents to consumer and societal well-being. Substantively, she specializes in issues related to medical decision making and sustainability in consumer and firm behavior. Prior to joining Boston University, she completed a Ph.D. in marketing at New York Universitys Stern School of Business. She completed her B.S. and M.S. (summa cum laude) at Bocconi University, her M.A. (Honors) in Psychology and her M. Phil. in Marketing both at New York University. Prior to joining academia, she worked in brand management for SC Johnson and Kraft Foods. .
