He holds M.Sc. and B.Sc. degree all in Electrical Engineering from Amirkabir University of Technology (Tehran Polytechnic), Iran. He has been involved in EU Celtic Plus project SooGREEN "Service Oriented Optimization of Green Mobile Networks" together with Nokia, Orange, and other European partners, with focus on energy efficiency of networks. His research interests include Wireless Communication, Mobile Cloud Computing, and energy efficient5G Network Architectures.
