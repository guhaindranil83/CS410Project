Faculty Chaim Wollstein , MD Professor, Department of Ophthalmology Professor in Neural Science Biomedical Engineering Professor Wollstein is part of theAdvanced OphthalmicImaging Laboratory (AOIL), whichconsists of a team of ophthalmologists, engineers, software specialists, statisticians, and trainees. In collaboration with his colleagues, Profs. Schuman, and Ishikawa, he focuses on the study of glaucoma, a leading cause of blindness in the world. This group of renowned researchers is developing advanced imaging tools to detect this debilitating disease, monitor its progression, and gain a better understanding ofits pathogenesis.
