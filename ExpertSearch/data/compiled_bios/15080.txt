Virginia Polytech Institute and State University, 1985 Research Interests Study of advanced material systems and developing new methods to characterize and to model material behavior. Her labs research objective is to characterize and model advanced materials systems, at scales spanning the range of molecular interactions, micromechanical and macroscopic behavior.
