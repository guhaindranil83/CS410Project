His research interests are in ancient Greek philosophy, particularly metaphysics, epistemology, and natural philosophy. His articles include The Argument from Relatives (OSAP 2012) and Aristotle and the Ancient Puzzle about Coming to Be (OSAP 2015). His book, Aristotle and the Eleatic One, was recently published by Oxford University Press.
